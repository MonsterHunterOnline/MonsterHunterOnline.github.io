Weapon Talents

The weapon talent tree unlocks at level 20. The "H" key opens up the interface and it is the last tab at the top. Each tier has 3 talents and only when you learn all 3 talents in the same tier can you unlock the next tier.
Talent scroll fragments (天赋书碎片） are needed to purchase talent scrolls (天赋书) from the "教官" NPC. The fastest way to get the fragments is to do the unstable quests that unlock at lv.20. You can learn more about unstable quests in the Quests section. you must complete the optional objective to get the fragment. You can also complete guild missions to gain the fragments. They are given randomly and may not be for your weapon. The final way to get them is to trade them with your friends or purchase them on the auction house.
