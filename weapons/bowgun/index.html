  • Weapon Info
    Tier 1 Talents
    1-1

    As long as stamina is enough, you can do Consecutive Side Steps, also shortens the time between each Side Step.

    1-2

    Side Step can cancel Charging Shot.

    1-3

    Side Step can cancel Rapid Shot before shooting the last bullet.

    Tier 2 Talents
    2-1

    Perfect Reload Time. The zone of Perfect Reload is increased significantly.

    2-2

    If your clip capacity for the ammo type >= 2 , perfect reload loads 2 red bullets instead of 1.

    2-3

    After being pushed back from shooting a bullet with large recoil, automatically backflip, pushing yourself even further back.

    Tier 3 Talents
    3-1

    When loading bullets, only perfect quality bullets are reloaded in the empty portion of the clip.

    3-2

    After launching all the perfect quality bullets in the current clip, reloading speed increased by 20% for 10 seconds.

    3-3

    Ultimate 1: increases on attaching bullet.
    Ultimate 2: Click "E" and if your attack hits the target it will increase the launching damage.
