  • Weapon Info
    Tier 1 Talents
    1-1

    旋子回避 could interrupt 鬼人连斩 and 鬼人乱舞 randomly.

    1-2

    The first stroke Left 回旋斩 and the first Right 回旋斩 could form combo.

    1-3

    Press R key when the 鬼人槽 is full to cast 落叶斩 and enter 鬼人化 at the same time.

    Tier 2 Talents
    2-1

    落叶斩 displacement +50% and gain 回避无敌 time.

    2-2

    Press R key while you are in 鬼人化and 鬼人狂舞 to cast 落叶斩.

    2-3

    After 鬼人狂舞, you could cast 贵人狂舞 and other basic skill again.

    Tier 3 Talents
    3-1

    After 鬼人化 duration time surpasses 9 second, press E to quit 鬼人化。 When you quit 鬼人化, you will come out with 鬼人气浪. When 鬼人气浪 hit the target, you could add 鬼人槽.

    3-2

    The duration time of 鬼人槽 could improve to 20 second.

    3-3

    The both of Ougi 1 and 2 could gain 鬼人化 debuff.
