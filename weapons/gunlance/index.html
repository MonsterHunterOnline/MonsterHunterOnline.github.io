    Tier 1 Talents
    1-1

    Can reload all shells after a Full Burst.

    1-2

    Can enable Heat Blade after Normal Shot, Charge Shot & Quick Reload.

    1-3

    Can Back/Side Step to cancel animation lock after shooting.

    Tier 2 Talents
    2-1

    Wyvern Fire charge time redudes by 30%.

    2-2

    After Fire Sweep, can use Slam Attack & Wyvern Fire.

    2-3

    Greatly reduce charge time of Charged Shot.

    Tier 3 Talents
    3-1

    After using Wyvern Fire, can perform Heat Gauge Filling to recover 75% of the gauge.

    3-2

    Reduces Heat Gauge consumption speed while Heat Blade is enabled by 30%.

    3-3

    When Heat Gauge is full, increase 1st Ultimate blast radius & 2nd Ultimate flame distance.
