  • Weapon Info
    Tier 1 Talents
    1-1

    When the Spirit Gauge is at level 1 or 2, and there's enough spirit, after the 2nd Spirit Slash, can press R to perform Spirit Refilling Slash.

    1-2

    Increases Fade Slash & Inverse Fade Slash travel distance by 50%.

    1-3

    After Inverse Fade Slash, can perform Fade Slash again.

    Tier 2 Talents
    2-1

    When Spirit Gauge level 3 drops, it'll return to level 2.

    2-2

    At Spirit Gauge level 3, can perform quickdraw Spirit Refilling Slash every 120s (When weapon is sheathed and has enough Spirit, press R).

    2-3

    After quickdraw Spirit Refilling Slash, as long as there's enough Spirit, can perform Giant Slash.

    Tier 3 Talents
    3-1

    If Extreme Giant Slash hit a target, increases Spirit Gauge level by 1.

    3-2

    Can roll to interupt Spirit Refilling Slash, Giant Slash & Extreme Giant Slash after sheathing animation.

    3-3

    Ultimate 1: Can gain a level of Spirit Gauge.
    Ultimate 2: The final strike can increase 3 levels of Spirit Gauge.
