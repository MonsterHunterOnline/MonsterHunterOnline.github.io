  • Weapon Info
    Tier 1 Talents
    1-1

    After Upward Swing & Turning Swing, can left+right click to perform Golf Swing.

    1-2

    Charge lv3 Spinning Attack travel speed increases by 100%.

    1-3

    Charge lv3 Spinning Attack animation speed increases by 50%.

    Tier 2 Talents
    2-1

    While moving with level 3 charge, can left click to perform Sweep Swing, then automatically enter Spinning Attack.

    2-2

    While moving with charge, can press Shilft to speed up movement.

    2-3

    When Superpound & Chain Superpound hit the ground, it'll release stun shockwave (only has stun value, but no physical damage).

    Tier 3 Talents
    3-1

    After Charge lv3 Spinning Attack, can perform 2 Golf Swing continuously.

    3-2

    After Superpound, can perform another Upward Swing.

    3-3

    Ultimate 1: While in Charge lv3, performing Ultimate will result in 2 ground bounching shockwaves.
    Ultimate 2: While in Charge lv3, performing Ultimate will expand ground collapsing area.
