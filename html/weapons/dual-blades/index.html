  • Weapon Info
    Tier 1 Talents
    1-1

    Demon Dodge can be used to cancel Demon's Dance combo

    1-2

    Left Spin & Right Spin Slash can form a combo.

    1-3

    When Demon Gauge is full, press R to perform Forward Jump Slash and enter Demonization mode at the same time.

    Tier 2 Talents
    2-1

    Increases Forward Jump Slash travel distance by 50% & gain invulnerable time.

    2-2

    While in Demonization mode & Demon's Dance, can press R to perform Forward Jump Slash.

    2-3

    After a Demon's Dance, can perform it again, or use other basic moves.

    Tier 3 Talents
    3-1

    When Demonization mode lasts over 9 seconds, press E to cancel it. Then it'll release a demon wave, if the wave hit a target, it'll recover demon gauge.

    3-2

    Increase demon gauge max duration to 20s.

    3-3

    Both 1st & 2nd Ultimate can obtain Demonization mode's damage increase.
